about

Welcome to Tiny Cakes. We take pride in our bold flavors. All of our ingredients are local, organic, and non-GMO. We are well known for our exotic flavors: mint, lavendar, cardemum, absinth (fairy kissed), and Mexican chocolate to name a few. Please keep in mind that we alternate our flavors monthly to use what's in season, and to keep you on your toes!

history

Michelle Beyder has been baking since the 80's. She learned her baking techniques from the ways of her Moroccan grandmother. Her early introduction to international ingredients lead her to this magical love affair for creating exotic and delicious cupcake making.
